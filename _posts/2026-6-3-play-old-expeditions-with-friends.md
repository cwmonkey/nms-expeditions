Instead of going fully offline, you can now use <a href="https://www.nexusmods.com/nomanssky/mods/4251" target="_blank">Grouch's "Offline Mode" mod</a> or <a href="https://nomansapp.com/transcender" target="_blank">Mjstral's Transcender .dll Plugin</a> to play past expeditions with friends.
